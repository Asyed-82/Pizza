📋 How It Works

  • Customer Page: For taking orders from customers
  • Kitchen Page: Opens in kitchen with Bluetooth printer
  • Auto-Print: New orders automatically print to TM-m30III
  • Real-time: Orders appear instantly in kitchen
  • Mobile Friendly: Works perfectly on phones/tablets

🖨️ Printer Setup (TM-m30III)

  • 1. Open Kitchen Dashboard on your kitchen mobile device
  • 2. Click "Connect Bluetooth Printer" button
  • 3. Select your TM-m30III from Bluetooth list
  • 4. All new orders will auto-print!
